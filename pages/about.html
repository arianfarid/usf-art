A Showcase of Art

This app was made by AIST of the University of South Florida to showcase and facilitate appreciation for the public art on the Tampa Campus, and to provide context for each of the pieces.


Though there are installations inside certain buildings, in order for a GPS-based app such as this to properly display information for such pieces, only the outside installations have been considered for the production of this app.


Where appropiate, information on each piece is cited at the bottom of the article. Varouis sources have been used, including electronic and physical resources.


For more information about AIST and what we do, please visit Our website for more information.
